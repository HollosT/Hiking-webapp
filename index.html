What routes are you looking for?

Bjergbanestien

1 walking route, 14km 1 biking path

Route:from Sventrup via Oldstien to Nibe/Godthaab

How to get there:take the train from the Aalborg station.

From Skalskoven (west from Nibe), the path continues along Nørregade, which leads to the center of Nibe, where Nibe railway station once stood. The path continues through Nibe and towards Valpsund along the regional cycle route 29 (dark blue color on the map).

Egholm

Four walking routes: 2.3km 5.3km 7.6km 8.8km
It is located on an island

Route:Egholm Island

How to get there:by ferry from Aalborg West

Egholm offers a lot of possibility during the hike or even after. Restaurants, caffes, or maybe kayaking. There is also hidden a troll somewhere on the island built from recycled wood. So lets go and find him! You can bring along your dog, but only on leash!

Oesteraadalen Nord

Three walking routes: 4.1km 2km 1km The routes enclose a fishing lakes for potential fishers

Route:it is in Aadalen.

How to get there:Only 3 km from Aalborg Centrum you will find Oesteraadalen Nord

In 1992 and in the following year a nature restoration project was carried out in Oesteraadalen Nord. The project was a huge success and this park was created in the middle of the big city. With an absolutely fantastic nature and recreational opportunities in the middle of Aalborg, Østerådalen is a popular excursion destination that provide possibilities to runners, bikers, walkers.

Oesteraadalen Syd

Two walking routes: 6.5km 5km Including bicycle route as well

Route:Walking and cycling route from Dall Moellevej in Svenstrup to Aalborg Centrum via Oesteraadalen Nord. There is only one path for walking porpuses.

Mulbjerge

With multiple little walks in the area

Route:The routes start at Stejlgabet.

How to get there:take the bus no. 56 from the busterminal

Visis Mulbjerge which used to be an island. It's coast offer great spots for the people who wnat to fish. It is a great place for family gatherings, together with children, there are a grill spot, bathing pier. You can also reach the highest spot of the region, along a paved path,above sea level. Here you will find the panoramic table which gives you an overview about the area. You can bring along your dog, but only on leash!

Poulstrup

Three walking routes: 6.3km 2.5km 2.5km With allocated horse riding route for riders

Route:Polstrup Lake and Dall Hede, 12 km from Aalborg

How to get there:Most easier by car.

Polstrup offers a track to the horse fanatics or to the currious spectators. It's lakes can give you the true nature experience including every beauty of it.
